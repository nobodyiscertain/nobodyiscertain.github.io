Hire me for your project

I have a decade of experience planning, building, crafting and
tweaking code to into fine web products. What can I do for you?

Who are you?

You've got an idea for an app and are ready to break ground to bring it to life.

You've got an existing app that needs new features, bug fixes and/or maintenance, and are looking for an experienced developer to help.

Who am I?

I am a full-stack web developer in Southern California. I've worked remotely for the last 7 years with clients from all country and even some parts of the world.

I love working with backend technologies like Ruby on Rails, Laravel, and front-end libraries like Vue.js and Backbone.

I'm scrappy, fast paced, and most importantly, if I agree to a scope of work, you can trust that it will get done. I fully understand the investment I am for your business and I take that seriously. From getting broken code back up and running, to bringing a new MVP into the world, I'm your development partner until the project is complete.

How's this going to work?

If you've got an existing system in place with your team, sweet! I'll adopt your work flow and fit right in.

Otherwise, I find it best to work in weekly iterations. If this is a new project, here's a rough idea of how it'll go down. At the beginning of each week, we'll meet and agree on a set of deliverables for the week. Then I'll go heads down all week, communicating progress through Basecamp. At the end of the week we'll meet to review, celebrate what's shipped, and plan for the next week. Then we do it all over again until we're finished.

What's my schedule?

I work Monday - Friday, 8am - 4pm, PST. I will be available for any correspondance during these hours. Prefereably through Basecamp or Slack.

I don't schedule any work during evenings or weekends.

What's my cost & availability?

I bill weekly at $3,200 per week and I have availability for a new project starting October 16. I prefer to schedule projects without any overlap.

If your project is a build from the ground up, you can buy a Roadmapping session with me for $1000. We'll spend a day realizing the scope of the project and get it all planned out. No user-story left untold. Then I'll put a proposal together based on the roadmap. (This also makes it much easier for you to get a second opinion.)

What's a normal engagement length?

For an existing app, this entirely depends on the scope of the work requested. It can last anywhire from 1-4 weeks or more. For brand new builds, these average 1-2 months.

There are no long term commitments. If at any time either of us is unsatisfied with how things are going, we can simply part ways.
